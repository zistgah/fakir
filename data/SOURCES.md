# Data sources (exhaustive lattice)
- **ISIC Rev.4** — UN Statistics Division, `ISIC_Rev_4_english_structure.Txt`
  (unstats.un.org/unsd/classifications/Econ). 21 sections · 88 divisions · 238 groups · 419 classes. Verbatim.
- **ISCO-08** — ILO, "ISCO-08 EN Structure and definitions" (ilo.org), ingested via the
  `reisfe/isco` package dataset. 10 major · 43 sub-major · 130 minor · 436 unit groups.
- **ISCED-F 2013** — UNESCO-UIS "Fields of Education and Training 2013", Appendix 1 code list.
  11 broad · 29 narrow · 80 detailed fields.
- **AGI stack L0–L9** — Project ILM (ilm.codes/explore).
Counts are hard-gated in `apply` and at build; classification content is © its issuing bodies and
reproduced for interoperability/reference. Lattice assembly © 1993–2026 Abhishek Choudhary (CC0-1.0 metadata).
